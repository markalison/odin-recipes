Sunrise Sunset Smoothie

ingredients

  • Half a pound of chopped mangoes
  • Half a pound of chopped pineapples
  • Half a pound of chopped strawberries
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup

steps

  1. The best way to get a good texture is to add the liquid ingredients first. Start pouring the orange juice and maple syrup into the blender. Give it a quick pulse so they combine well.
  2. Next add the mango chunks, strawberries, and pineapple pieces to the blender. The secret here is to let the frozen fruit sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes before you get started. This will make them blend better.
  3. Add some ice cubes to make a more chilled smoothie (optional)
  4. Blend on low and then high speed until itâ€™s smooth and creamy. To adjust the texture, add some more orange juice or fruits. But if it still turns out thinner than you expected, just blend in a banana. It works every time.
  5. Serve
